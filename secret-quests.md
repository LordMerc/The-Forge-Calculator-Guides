# Monkey Bananite

There is a quest hidden on the island of the **2nd world** right behind the spawning point.

1. Where the mouse is pointed, head here
   ![Image|100%](https://i.vgy.me/mB3Ftm.jpg)
2. Speak to the monkey
   ![Image|100%](https://i.vgy.me/BSan4x.jpg)
3. He will ask for Bananite, choose to give him 30
