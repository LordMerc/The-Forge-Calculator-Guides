## Tips for Using These Builds

1. **Trait Thresholds** - Remember ores need to be at least 10% of total to activate their trait
2. **Scaling** - Trait effectiveness scales from 10% (at 10% ore) to 100% (at 30%+ ore)
3. **Balance** - Higher multiplier doesn't always mean better - traits matter!
4. **Adapt** - These are guidelines; adjust based on what ores you have available
